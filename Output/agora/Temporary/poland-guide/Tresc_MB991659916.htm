Zajazd Wilk i Orka

 

"Zajazd Wilk i Orka" is a rustic fish restaurant known for its focus on fresh, sweet-water fish, offering guests an authentic taste of Poland. Located in a picturesque countryside setting, the restaurant blends traditional charm with a cozy, homely atmosphere, complete with wooden accents and a warm, welcoming vibe.

Menu

The menu features a variety of sweet-water fish dishes, such as trout, perch, and pike, all sourced from local rivers and lakes. Each dish is carefully prepared to bring out the natural sweetness and delicate flavors of these freshwater fish, often accompanied by seasonal vegetables and traditional Polish side dishes.
